Our Recipes



Green Team

Prep: 10min > Ready in: 10min

  • 20g pinapple chunks
  • 135g chopped kale
  • 40g cucumber
  • 30g celery
  • 5g ginger
  • 75ml coconut milk

Method

Place the ingredients into a blender. Cover and blend for at least 1 minute. Then serve and enjoy.
